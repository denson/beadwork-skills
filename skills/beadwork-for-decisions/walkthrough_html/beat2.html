Beat 2 · How decisions work in bw

A decision is just a ticket with a specific shape.

Standard ADR format maps onto bw's existing shape. No new tooling, no new schema, no separate decision-log database.

Decision = ticket. ADR shape, bw substrate.

  • Title: the decision (e.g. "Auth: Auth0 vs in-house OAuth").
  • Description: the question + the options being considered.
  • Comments: the discussion — different voices, signed and timestamped as the conversation evolves over hours, days, weeks.
  • Close-comment: the verdict + the rationale, written by whoever made the call.
  • Status: open while discussing → closed when decided.

Six months later, the next person runs bw show <ticket-id> and gets the entire decision thread back — question, options, discussion, verdict, rationale — in chronological order, every voice attributed.

bw was always this shape. You just use it for decisions deliberately.
