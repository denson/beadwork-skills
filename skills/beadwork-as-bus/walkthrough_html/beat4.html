beadwork-as-bus · Beat 4 · Close

You have a team.

The shift to take away: with bw, your AI isn't one amnesiac generalist anymore. It's one member of a small team of specialist agents — design, implementation, review, audit — that share a durable, signed, auditable workspace. Most of the time those agents are sessions of one lab's model (all Claude, or all Gemini), each playing a different role.

Two extras the substrate gives you for free, both worth knowing about:

  • Humans on a team can join the same workspace. If you push bw's orphan branch to a shared private repo, your teammates' agents (and the teammates themselves) read and write the same store.
  • Cross-lab works too. Because bw is just shell commands, an agent from a different vendor — Gemini reading a store Claude agents have been writing to — participates the same way. Most users won't need this; it's a nice insurance policy if you ever swap labs.
