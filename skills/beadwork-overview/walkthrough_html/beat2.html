Before going deeper — how do you mainly use AI?

The rest of this tour is going to look different depending on your background. There's a coder version (assumes you know what git is, what a repo is, what a CLI is) and an everyone-else version (explains all of that in plain English). Same tool, same story, just different vocabulary.

Writing code Software development, scripts, infra, automation.
Writing, research, learning Drafting, analysis, study, summaries.
Business or personal projects Operations, customers, finance, legal, life admin.
Something else A mix, or none of the above.

Pick whichever feels closest. The next page splits two ways: "writing code" goes to the SWE-flavored version; everything else goes to the plain-English version.
