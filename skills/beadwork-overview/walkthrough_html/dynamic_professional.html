Path C · Independent / professional services

bw as personal-OS substrate.

Lawyers and solo entrepreneurs share a third shape: small team or solo, audit-critical, no admin overhead to set up. They wear many hats; their work needs to be defensible; they don't have a team's worth of specialized tools.

Personal-OS, audit-grade, low overhead.

Lawyer / paralegal

Each case is a ticket epic; child tickets per phase (discovery, deposition prep, filing, settlement). A new associate joining the case reads bw show for the full case history — no months of catch-up reading.

A discovery dump arrives: 10,000 documents. Agents partition the work. The lawyer runs an audit agent over the bw history to verify inter-agent consistency. Bar standing rides on review accuracy — the audit agent is professional insurance.

Privilege log preserved with timestamp + drafter + rationale. Opposing counsel challenges a redaction six months later — bw show produces court-defensible reasoning.

Privacy is non-negotiable. Client confidentiality means bw stays local-only or in a strictly-private repo. The git-native trust story isn't nice-to-have here — it's the only reason a SaaS-based memory tool would be a non-starter.

Solo entrepreneur / small agency

Every domain — sales, ops, finance, marketing, knowledge — gets a bw epic. The AI logs activity, decisions, vendor notes across all of them. One assistant, one repo, six domains, durable memory across all of it. No separate Notion + spreadsheet + email + Slack mess.

Lessons-learned across engagements: "you tried this with Acme; didn't work because X. Want a different approach?" Personal knowledge graph that survives every chat compaction and laptop swap.

Tax-time defense: AI runs bw show on the year's expense decisions — auditable history without separate accounting software.

Common thread: bw as personal-OS substrate. Durable, auditable, no admin overhead, privacy-respecting. Works whether you're a 20-year solo lawyer or a one-person consulting agency.
