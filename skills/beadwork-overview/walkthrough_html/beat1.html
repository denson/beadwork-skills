Beat 1 · The hook

Your AI assistant forgets. bw fixes that.

Chat ends, sessions compact, machines change — the thread your AI was on is gone, and tomorrow's session starts blank. You spend the first ten minutes re-explaining context you already explained yesterday.

Sessions end. Context shouldn't.

There's a small tool called bw — beadwork, written by jallum — that gives your AI durable memory and a shared communication channel with other AIs. It works across every kind of work: engineering, science, operations, customer support, legal, solo business. It works across every AI vendor: Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, anything that can run a small CLI command.

The interaction is simple: you talk to your agent; the agent uses bw; you don't have to know it's there.

Tomorrow's session opens, your AI runs bw show on whatever you were working on, and continues from where you left off.
